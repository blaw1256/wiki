The Nation of Ipata

General Information

The Nation of Ipata is the most southern landmass in Ariaunus. In terms of land surface area Ipata is the second largest landmass, second only to Bruxduvia. Ipata's closest neighbour is Droomadia but economically its ties to Troson are much stronger, having been one of the few places to not interrupt trade to Troson following the rebellion.
